Education Quality and Access in Liberia (EQUAL)

Education Quality and Access in Liberia (EQUAL). The four year EQUAL program, supported by USAID and implemented by Concern Worldwide, in partnership with LIBTRALO and the Ministry of Education, aims to improve equitable access and early grade literacy and numeracy in 100 primary schools in Grand Bassa County.

Improve early childhood education, primary education, and secondary education, delivered in formal or non-formal settings. It includes literacy, numeracy, and other basic skills programs for youth and adults.
