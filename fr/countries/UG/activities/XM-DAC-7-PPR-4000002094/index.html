NUTRITION AND INCOME GENERATION INTERV.

This project works within the agro sector and aims at contrib-uting to healthier lives and more resilient livelihoods of refu-gees and hosts in the areas of West Nile Region of Uganda, which struggles with a shortage of locally produced nutritious food. The intervention focuses on improving access to and con-sumption of nutritious crops, and increase income for refugees and hosts in refugee settlement areas in West Nile Region. A consortium including a link with the private sector aims to ensure that the agro sector is sustainably transformed. Inter-ventions focus on: 1. System innovation: development of innovative activi-ties to improve access and consumption in protracted refugee situations.
