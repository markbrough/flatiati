Social Impact Bond Pilot -Sleeping sickness in Uganda

To investigate whether a payment by result mechanism (a social bond) can deliver (i) greater benefits so as to significantly reduce the risk of sleeping sickness; and (ii) provide an incentive to innovate area livestock treatment to manage prevalence of sleeping sickness in people.
