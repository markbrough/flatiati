Regional Health Integration to Enhance Services in Eastern Region (RHITES-E)

(Uganda): Regional Health Integration to Enhance Services in Eastern Uganda (RHITES-E) supports the Government of Uganda and key stakeholders to increase availability and utilization of high quality health services in 21 districts in Eastern Uganda and two districts in Karamoja. This goal will be achieved through: health systems strengthening; and the improvement of quality, access and demand for health services with attention to poverty and underserved populations (e.g., youth and adolescent girls, key populations, women). The activity will support the health sector to sustain higher service utilization by supporting quality integration of services including HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis, maternal, newborn and child health, reproductive health, nutrition and malaria.
