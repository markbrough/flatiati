Performance and Impact Evaluation of Literacy and Health Education Program

(Uganda): The LARA P&IE service contract will provide technical assistance to conduct a performance and impact evaluation of the five-year Literacy Achievement and Retention Activity (LARA) in Uganda (RFA 617-15-00009). One major purpose of this performance and impact evaluation is to explore the extent to which intended outcomes are being achieved, and the effectiveness of different program approaches and interventions. The impact evaluation will test the extent to which the program's activities improve reading skills and retention in primary grades. A particular focus will be the activity's interventions that address student safety and school-related gender-based violence (SRGBV), and to what extent these interventions result in measurable impact on student learning, specifically early grade reading.
