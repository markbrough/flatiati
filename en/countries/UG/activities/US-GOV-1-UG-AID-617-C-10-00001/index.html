Northern Uganda Development of Enhanced Local Governance, Infrastructure, and Livelihoods (NUDEIL) Project

Northern Uganda Development of Enhanced Local Governance Infrastructure and Livelihoods (NUDEIL): A Northern Uganda program intended to support the Government of Uganda (GOU) through the provision of technical support to improve district government capacity in engineering design, procurement process, financial mangement, tranparent reporting and public outreach.
