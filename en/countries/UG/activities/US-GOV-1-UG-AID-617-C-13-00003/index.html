Feed the Future Commodity Production and Marketing activity

(Uganda): The objective of the activity is to improve competitiveness of the coffee, maize, and bean value chains by achieving sustainable increases in the production and marketing of high-quality maize, beans, and coffee in 34 Feed the Future focus districts. The expected results include: increased crop productivity; increased availability and effectiveness of support services; strengthened inter-actor relationships for better movement of products and information between buyers and sellers; and increased access to competitive markets. ...
