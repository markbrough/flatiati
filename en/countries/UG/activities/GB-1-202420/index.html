Accelerating the Rise in Contraceptive Prevalence in Uganda

To increase the use of modern methods of contraception in Uganda through proven non-state sector family planning service delivery channels such as outreach services, social marketing and social franchising. This will enable over 430,000 additional Ugandan women to access family planning services between 2011 and 2015.
